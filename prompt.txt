Based on everything I've seen building this project, here's what I'd prioritize:

**Conversion & Trust**
- No social proof on the homepage — add a live "X orders delivered today" counter or recent purchase notifications
- Product pages have no "frequently bought together" or upsell
- The checkout has no trust badges (SSL, instant delivery, money-back) right before the pay button — that's where people abandon
- No guest checkout email confirmation — guests can't find their order later

**UX gaps**
- Search page probably returns no results for typos — needs fuzzy search or at least category filters
- Wishlist exists but there's no "notify me when back in stock" for out-of-stock products
- No order tracking page that auto-refreshes — customers have to manually reload the invoice
- The deals page countdown timer resets on refresh

**Revenue**
- No upsell at checkout success — "customers also bought" after purchase
- Discount codes can't be stacked or have minimum order values
- No referral reward for the person being referred (only the referrer earns)
- Partner affiliate commission isn't actually credited anywhere in the checkout flow — the code exists but `creditAffiliateCommission` only handles promo affiliates, not partner affiliates

**Operations**
- Partner affiliate commission crediting is missing — when a `pref=` referral code is used at checkout, nothing credits the partner's balance
- No email when a partner payout is approved/rejected
- Staff can't see order details, only the list — no drill-down

**SEO**
- Blog posts have no internal linking to products
- No structured data on the homepage
- Product images don't have proper alt text from the product title
